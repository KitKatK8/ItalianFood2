Italian Chopped Salad

Enjoy this refreshing and easy salad with your favorite Italian entrees.

Ingredients

  • 1 Romaine Heart (3 cups)
  • 5 cups leafy lettuce
  • 1 large shallot
  • 1 English cucumber (2 cups chopped)
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup ripe green olives, halved
  • 1/2 cup jarred sliced pepperoncini
  • 1/4 cup Parmesan shavings or shreds, plus more to serve
  • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes, optional
  • 1/2 cup Italian Dressing of your choice

Steps

  1. Chop lettuce. Slice the shallot. Peel and chop cucumber. Slice tomatoes and olives in half
  2. Mix all ingredients, including the dressing, and toss to combine.
